Step 1: Solidify your structure.


For four weeks, we do a one-hour weekly call to work on progressing your manuscript forward, regardless of your starting point. We start by focusing on your manuscript's structure, marketing with respect to title and cover content, and meeting your goals for the book as a whole.


Step 2: Find the right words.


The following four weeks, we zoom in to the text in our weekly calls. With your structure in place, we can ensure the best possible use of vocabulary, grammar, and style, among many other elements, to captivate your target reader.

During this phase, a bit of time apart from our calls will be required of you to complete my writing prompts for certain pieces of your book. Additional support during this phase is also provided such as the opportunity for texts and audio messages.


Step 3: Tie up loose ends.


Revising what you wrote is key for saving time and money during the editing process! For the last four weeks, we do weekly final checks together before the manuscript is ready to be edited.

We may move paragraphs and sentences around to best fit your intended message. This phase focuses on clarity and cutting away extra text that may distract from the main message (you can always save unused content for your next book!).
