Hi! I'm Sandra Wissinger.

I am a professional proofreader from West Palm Beach, Florida. I am Colombian-American. I love academic proofreading and also offer commercial and creative proofreading. I have experience with basic copyediting (especially for speakers of English as a second language) and can translate works from English to Spanish and vice versa. I have a background in classical, jazz, and punk vocal performance and education. The majority of my work experience has been in the dog walking, dog sitting, and pet boarding industries, as well as office assistance and customer service. I am an English nerd and love writing essays. My hobbies include painting, drawing, reading, writing, photography, Latin dancing, learning about history, and listening to operas.
