Hi! I'm Sandi.

I'm a writing coach from South Florida. I work with fiction and non-fiction authors alike. My background in writing, developmental editing, line editing, and copyediting allow me to help my authors hone in on their target audience to keep their ideal reader engaged and loyal.

Together, we edit or create a manuscript you can be proud of. I provide insider tips like how to make sure you don't run into technical issues throughout your indie author path, how to save money and time on editing, and clear steps for a smooth publishing process.

My calling is to help people find their voice. My background as a vocal teacher is my strength in encouraging my authors to not stop writing and to simplify the process to get the results they seek.

My Signature 3-Step Process™

Coaching Package


$1080

Step 1: Solidify your structure.


For four weeks, we do a one-hour weekly call to work on progressing your manuscript forward, regardless of your starting point. We start by focusing on your manuscript's structure, marketing with respect to title and cover content, and meeting your goals for the book as a whole. Chapters may move around, and titles and subtitles may change. It’s all part of the process!


Step 2: Find the right words.


The following four weeks, we zoom in to the text in our weekly calls. During this phase, a bit of time apart from our calls will be required of you to complete my writing prompts for certain pieces of your book. Additional support during this phase is also provided such as texts and audio messages.

With your structure in place, we can ensure the best possible use of vocabulary, grammar, and style, among many other elements to captivate your target reader. We may move paragraphs and sentences around to best fit your intended message. This phase focuses on clarity and cutting away extra text that may distract from the main message (you can always save unused content for your next book!).


Step 3: Tie up loose ends.


Revising what you wrote is key for saving time and money during the editing process! For the last four weeks, we do weekly final checks together before the manuscript is ready to be sent off to an editor. Don't worry—I can recommend someone awesome!
