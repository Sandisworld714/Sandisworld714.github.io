Nonfiction

My ideal client is someone who has a business book, nonfiction book, or memoir & they want to make sure it doesn't come off dry. I've been a huge business/self-help/leadership book nerd since I was 12! My life in the fine arts has shown me the power of story.

⮑ Nonfiction Genres: Academic, Autobiography, Biography, Business, Creative Nonfiction, DIY, History, How-to, Memoir, New Age, Reference, Self-Help, Textbooks

Fiction

When I edit fiction, I think of it in terms of cinematography. My ideal fiction client is someone who wants to strengthen their character development and dialogue. My background in acting & theater enables me to identify where authors can raise the stakes to keep their reader hooked and where they can make their characters more realistic, thus more relatable—especially "the bad guy."

⮑ Fiction Genres: Drama, Fantasy, Historical, Romance, Science Fiction

☕🍵 I edit and proofread both US & UK English.
