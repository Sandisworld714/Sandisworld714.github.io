Services for Self-Publishing Authors

Developmental editing


This phase is also referred to as heavy editing. It focuses on the manuscript's structure, marketing with respect to title and cover content, and meeting the author’s goals for the book as a whole. Chapters may move around, and titles and subtitles may change. It’s all part of the process!


$0.031 per word


Copy + line edit package


Our combined copy + line editing package ensures the best possible use of vocabulary, grammar, and style, among many other elements. We may move paragraphs and sentences around to best fit your intended message. This phase focuses on clarity and cutting away extra text that may distract from the main message (you can always save unused content for your next book!).


$0.041 per word


Proofreading


Proofreading is not to be confused with editing, although it often is! Proofreading is the final check before the manuscript is ready for formatting. This phase will ensure no errors, typos, inconsistencies, or misuse of words, among many other things. Our motto is: “Proofreading makes perfect!”


$0.021 per word
